What is the Guard Position?

    • Image by BLC

    The guard is a groundfigting position which allows a person, lying beneath their opponent to gain leverage for the purposes of applying a submission (choke or joint lock), or reversing the position by what is known as a sweep.

    A closed guard implies the person on bottom having their legs crossed behind their opponent, as opposed to an open guard
